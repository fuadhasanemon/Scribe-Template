The Future Of Transcribing

Skribe empowers you to easily capture witness testimony or interviews using online video and provides instant, post-event access to the video, exhibits and an A.I. transcript.

The Problem We
Intend To Solve

The legal industry is stuck in the Stone Age. Each year 3+ million deposition transcripts are typed by hand. Millions more are typed in trials, hearings, recorded statements (e.g., insurance claims) and other proceedings.

These transcripts are manually processed and delivered using outdated methods. Even though video is more persuasive, less than 5% of depositions are videoed. Why? Because attorneys need a transcript and they don’t want to pay for a court reporting firm, stenographer and videographer in a market rampant with middlemen and inflated costs. What if they didn’t have to?

About Us

Skribe replaces stenographers with video and A.I. transcripts, disrupting the $5 billion deposition and transcription market. By doing so, Skribe drastically lowers the cost of litigation and improves the user experience.

For the past 150+ years, the legal record has been hand typed by stenographers, a slow and expensive process. Now, we can do better. Instead of reading a transcript of what one person heard, Skribe captures video and delivers an A.I. transcript in a powerful web app. The recording is always instantly accessible and verifiable. Skribe eliminates the need for stenographers and, by doing so, reduces litigation expenses by more than $1 billion per year. We believe technology is good and can not only improve our productivity, but also the quality of our work and our lives. We sacrifice nothing in our pursuit of a better, more creative user experience. We provide customers with software that empowers them to use testimony in ways they’ve never imagined.

Skribe works with law firms, courts, claims & insurance companies, law enforcement and other professionals to improve the experience of capturing, processing and delivering witness testimony, such as depositions, hearings, trials, interviews and recorded statements. Our founder is an attorney with 15+ years of experience taking depositions and recorded statements for his clients, including numerous Fortune 500 companies.
